Company Introduction

TEMPEL GROUP is international company with more than 25 years working with Advantech. We are industrial solutions, monitoring, control and communications leaders in markets like Industrial Automation, Transportation, OIL&GAS, energy and so on.

TEMPEL works to provide our customers not only products but add value services, working with Advantech, the market leader, and providing all our knowledge to fulfill their needs and provide best solution for different projects. All our Staff, sales and technical, will help you to find the most effective product for your applications.
